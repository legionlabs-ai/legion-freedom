## Operating Philosophy

Every project must answer yes to most of the following questions before receiving significant investment:

- Can this generate revenue?
- Can AI automate most of the work?
- Can it scale?
- Can it be repeated?
- Will it strengthen the Legion Labs ecosystem?
- Will it create long-term value?
- Will it create measurable customer value?
- Will it save meaningful time?
- Will the results compound over time?

If the answer is consistently no, the opportunity should either be improved or moved to the backlog.

Legion Labs builds systems—not one-time wins.

Every asset should become part of a larger ecosystem that increases in value through research, execution, performance data, and continuous improvement.

# Phase 1 Operating Focus

Legion Labs is currently in Phase 1: Affiliate Revenue Engine.

During this phase, every engineering decision, AI employee, workflow, and business initiative should directly contribute to building a profitable affiliate media business.

Priority is given to initiatives that:

- Generate affiliate commissions.
- Produce high-quality YouTube and TikTok Shop content.
- Improve the speed or quality of content production.
- Increase audience growth and engagement.
- Improve conversion rates.
- Strengthen the AI workforce's ability to operate with minimal human intervention.

Projects that do not directly support the Affiliate Revenue Engine should be deferred to the product backlog unless approved by the CEO.

The objective of Phase 1 is to prove that an AI workforce can consistently generate real-world revenue through content-driven affiliate marketing.
