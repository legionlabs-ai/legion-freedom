## Operating Philosophy

Every project must answer yes to most of the following questions before receiving significant investment:

- Can this generate revenue?
- Can AI automate most of the work?
- Can it scale?
- Can it be repeated?
- Will it strengthen the Legion Labs ecosystem?
- Will it create long-term value?
- Will it create measurable customer value?
- Will it save meaningful time?
- Will the results compound over time?

If the answer is consistently no, the opportunity should either be improved or moved to the backlog.

Legion Labs builds systems—not one-time wins.

Every asset should become part of a larger ecosystem that increases in value through research, execution, performance data, and continuous improvement.
