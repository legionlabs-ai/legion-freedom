## Primary Revenue Stream

Legion Labs' first revenue stream is affiliate commerce.

The Legion Labs AI workforce will identify promising products, research consumer demand, analyze competition, create high-quality content, publish across multiple platforms, and measure campaign performance.

### Distribution Channels

- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube
- YouTube Shorts
- Facebook
- Pinterest
- Blogs
- Email newsletters

### Affiliate Platforms

- Amazon Associates
- TikTok Shop Affiliate
- Impact
- CJ Affiliate
- ShareASale
- Individual brand affiliate programs

Revenue is earned when customers purchase products through Legion Labs affiliate links.

The objective is to build a repeatable system where AI continuously discovers products, creates content, publishes at scale, measures results, and applies those lessons to future campaigns.

## Opportunity Prioritization

Priority is given to opportunities that:

- Generate revenue quickly
- Require minimal human involvement after launch
- Can be automated by the AI workforce
- Scale without proportionally increasing costs
- Produce recurring or compounding revenue
- Can be replicated across industries
- Strengthen the Legion Labs ecosystem

Legion Labs prioritizes systems, not one-time wins.
